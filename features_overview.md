### 🔗 Feature #15 — Collaborative Business Glossary & Catalog Enrichment

**What it is & why it matters:**
While Unity Catalog provides technical metadata, enriching it with business context such as definitions, ownership, PII status, and data quality scores is often a manual and fragmented process. This feature will overlay a collaborative business glossary on top of Unity Catalog assets, enabling users to easily define terms, assign ownership, add custom tags, integrate data quality metrics, and foster collaboration directly within the lineage tool.
