## 📅 2026-04-05

### 🔗 Feature #11 — Interactive Impact Analysis & Simulation

**What it is & why it matters:**
Data teams find it challenging to predict the downstream consequences of schema changes, data quality issues, or deprecating a dataset. This feature will offer an interactive graph view to visualize all affected downstream tables, reports, dashboards, and ML models, coupled with a 'what-if' simulation capability to preview the potential impact of a proposed change before implementation, minimizing risks.

**How to use it:**

See `tests/test_impact_simulator.py` for usage examples.
